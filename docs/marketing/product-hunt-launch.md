## Product Hunt

**Name:** ResumeAI-Bot

**Tagline (≤60 chars):**
- A) `AI builds your resume & auto-applies to jobs in 50+ countries`
- B) `Auto-apply to jobs worldwide with an AI-tailored resume`

**Topics:** Job Search, Artificial Intelligence, Productivity, Career

**Description (≤260 chars):**
> ResumeAI-Bot tailors your resume to each role and auto-submits applications to
> openings across 50+ countries — no job-board passwords required. Free tier (3
> apps/day), 30-day money-back guarantee. Built for job seekers going global, not
> just US LinkedIn.

**First / maker comment:**
> Hey Product Hunt 👋 I'm the (solo) founder.
>
> I built ResumeAI-Bot because applying to jobs abroad is brutal: you have to
> tailor a resume to every role, find each country's job boards, and apply at a
> volume that's exhausting by hand. Most auto-apply tools are US/LinkedIn-only —
> and some ask for your LinkedIn password, which is an account-ban risk.
>
> So this works differently:
> • AI rewrites your resume per role so it passes the ATS
> • It auto-submits to real application forms across 50+ countries
> • It never asks for or stores your job-board passwords
> • Free to start (3 applications/day), 30-day money-back on paid plans
>
> I'd love brutally honest feedback — especially from anyone who's job-hunted
> internationally. What would make you trust a tool to apply *for* you? 🙏
> (PH launch offer: 40% off the first year with code LAUNCH40.)

**Pre-written FAQ replies (paste as needed):**
- *Do you store my passwords?* No. We fill public application forms with your
  resume + a dedicated ResumeAI email. LinkedIn is the only optional path that
  needs a login.
- *Which countries/boards?* 50+ countries, sourcing from major boards and company
  career sites. The free plan covers a subset; paid expands it.
- *Is it just spam?* No — the resume is tailored to each role's keywords. Volume
  *with* targeting is the point.
- *Refund?* 30-day money-back, no questions, on every paid plan.
- *Pricing?* Free (3/day) · Pro $19.99/mo or $199/yr · Unlimited $29.99/mo or $299/yr.
