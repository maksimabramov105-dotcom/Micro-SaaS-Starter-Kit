> never-hard. Personalize line 1 to their actual post. ~10–20/day. Disclose
> you're the founder. Send the free teardown — that's the hook, not the sale.

> Saw your post — that grind is soul-crushing, sorry. 9/10 times 0 callbacks =
> ATS keyword mismatch, not you. Want me to run a free AI teardown of your resume?
> I'll send back an ATS score + the keywords you're missing for your target role.
> No strings — I build a resume tool and want feedback. Happy to just help.

**2. X DM — someone tweeting about applying abroad**
> Applying abroad is a different game (localization + volume). Happy to send a
> free teardown of your resume tuned for {country} if useful — I built a tool that
> does multi-country auto-apply, but genuinely just offering the teardown.

> tailors resumes per role + auto-applies across countries; before any of that,
> want a free teardown of your current resume? I'll flag the top 3 fixes.

> Sent your teardown 👆 The keyword gaps were the big ones. If you want the
> applying part automated (it tailors + submits across 50+ countries, free tier),

> automated teardown? (founder, no pressure.)

**6. CS new-grad, "is auto-apply worth it?"**

**7. Bootcamp grad / career switcher**
