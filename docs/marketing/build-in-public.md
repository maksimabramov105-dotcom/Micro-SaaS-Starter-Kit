## Lessons / value (these travel even with no metrics)
8. Cold traffic to an unknown AI tool is *deeply* skeptical — rightly. The single
   biggest conversion lever wasn't copy, it was adding a **30-day money-back
   guarantee**. Reduce risk → people try.
9. SEO reality check I had to accept: programmatic SEO is a 2–6 month play, not a
   30-day one. So month 1 is 100% manual distribution. Build the SEO engine now,
   harvest later.
10. Auto-apply tooling 101: if it doesn't *tailor the resume per role*, it's just
    spam with extra steps. ATS filters on keywords. Tailoring is the product.
11. Underrated market: people relocating / applying abroad. Every US-centric tool
    ignores them. That's my whole wedge.
12. The "data safety" objection is #1 for any auto-apply tool. I answer it head-on
    on the site: no job-board passwords stored, ever. Say the scary part out loud.
